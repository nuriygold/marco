# US Resident Programs Catalog

Eligibility: US resident or US-registered entity. Federal programs often require EIN. Some require US citizenship.

| Program | What You Get | Eligibility Gate | Value | Deadline | Link |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|-------|----------|------|
| SBIR Phase I (NSF) | Non-dilutive R&D grant | US small business, < 500 employees | $275,000 | Rolling (3x/yr) | https://www.nsf.gov/eng/iip/sbir/ |
| SBIR Phase II (NSF) | Continued R&D | Must complete Phase I | $1,000,000 | Post-Phase I | https://www.nsf.gov/eng/iip/sbir/ |
| STTR (NSF) | R&D with university partner | US small biz + research institution | $275,000–$1M | Rolling | https://www.nsf.gov/eng/iip/sbir/ |
| DOE SBIR/STTR | Energy-focused R&D | Energy sector startup | Up to $1.15M | Annual | https://science.osti.gov/sbir |
| NIH SBIR | Health/biotech R&D | US small business | Up to $1.15M | 3x/yr | https://grants.nih.gov/grants/funding/sbir.htm |
| DARPA BAA | Defense R&D contracts | US company | $1M–$50M | Rolling | https://www.darpa.mil/work-with-us/opportunities |
