# Mode: contacto

LinkedIn power move: find relevant contacts at a target company and draft outreach.

## Input

User provides: company name (and optionally a role/JD).

## Steps

1. **Identify target contacts** — suggest searching LinkedIn for:
   - Engineering managers or directors in the relevant team
   - Recruiters (Technical Recruiter, Talent Acquisition)
   - Senior/Staff engineers with similar stack
   - Alumni from candidate's previous companies now at target

2. **Draft connection request message** (300 chars max, LinkedIn limit):
   ```
   Hi [Name], I came across your profile while researching [Company].
   I'm a [candidate title] with [X years] in [key stack].
   Exploring opportunities in [space] — would love to connect.
   ```

3. **Draft follow-up InMail / message** (longer, after connecting):
   - 3 paragraphs: intro → specific interest in their work/team → ask
   - Personalize to their LinkedIn activity or public projects if possible
   - Clear ask: 15-min call, or "happy to share my resume if there's a fit"

4. **Referral angle** — if candidate has mutual connections, note them and suggest:
   "Ask [mutual] for a warm intro before cold outreach."

## Output

Print both messages (connection request + follow-up) ready to copy-paste.
Include tips on timing (morning weekdays perform best for InMail).
